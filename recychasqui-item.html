Recychasqui

Let's go to recycle!

Recychasqui is a platform that will allow people to collaborate in the management of waste management in their community.

The platform is focused on uniting people with their local government in the search for better waste management, through the organization of companies specialized in waste management.

The platform allows people to register in the waste management program and in this way one of the companies specialized in waste management is responsible for the collection.

Continuing with the process, the local government is responsible for encouraging the registration of people through various competitions or activities for the benefit of the community.
