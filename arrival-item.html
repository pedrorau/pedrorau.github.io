Arrival

Controlling attendance is our job!

Arrival is a platform that will allow better management of attendance control for both companies and their employees.

This platform is focused on the automation of the attendance control process essentially, as well as the processes of taking vacations and permits.

The platform allows the employees to register their attendance, as well fill out vacations and permits forms.

Likewise, the client module will allow to monitor and customize the attendance control configuration, in addition to accepting or denying the vacation and permits forms.
